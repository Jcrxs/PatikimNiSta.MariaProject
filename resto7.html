Welcome to Jacob's CAFE & RESTO

Discover a culinary haven in the heart of Sta. Maria, Bulacan, where flavors come alive and moments turn into cherished memories. Welcome to Jacob's CAFE & RESTO, a cozy retreat that invites you to indulge in the art of good food and warm hospitality.

Savor Delightful Moments at Jacob's CAFE & RESTO in Sta. Maria, Bulacan!


A Culinary Adventure Awaits:


At Jacob's, we believe in creating more than just meals – we craft experiences that resonate with your taste buds and capture the essence of heartfelt dining. Our diverse menu promises a symphony of flavors, offering something for every palate:

Gourmet Coffee Blends:
Begin your day or unwind with a cup of our meticulously brewed coffee blends. From robust espressos to velvety lattes, our coffee menu is a journey for coffee connoisseurs.

Delectable All-Day Dining:
Explore a menu that spans breakfast to dinner, featuring a delightful selection of appetizers, hearty mains, and satisfying desserts. Each dish is a testament to our commitment to quality and flavor.

Charming Dessert Delights: Indulge your sweet tooth with our tempting array of desserts. From classic treats to innovative creations, our desserts are the perfect finale to your dining experience.

The Place

Warm Ambiance, Inviting Atmosphere:

Step into Jacob's CAFE & RESTO and be embraced by the warmth of our ambiance. The cozy interiors and welcoming atmosphere provide the perfect setting for intimate conversations, family gatherings, or solo moments of reflection.





Visit us at:

1166 Km. 38 Pulong Buhangin Sta. Maria, Santa Maria, Philippines




Operating Hours

Jacob's CAFE & RESTO is more than a place to eat; it's a destination where every dish tells a story and every sip is a moment to savor. Join us and experience the joy of delightful dining at Jacob's – where good food meets genuine hospitality.
