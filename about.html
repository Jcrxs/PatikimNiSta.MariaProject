Meet the Team


The Culinary Adventurers Behind Patikim ni Sta. Maria

Welcome to "Patikim ni Sta.Maria," your ultimate guide to the diverse and delicious culinary scene in Sta. Maria, Bulacan. Our blog is dedicated to exploring and showcasing the rich flavors, mouth-watering dishes, and the vibrant restaurant culture in our beloved town.Allow us to introduce the minds and taste buds behind the scenes:

John Carlo Roxas - Chief Explorer

Meet John Carlo, the mastermind and Chief Explorer of Patikim ni Sta. Maria. With an insatiable appetite for culinary discoveries, John Carlo is the driving force behind our blog. His love for the town's rich cultural heritage and its diverse food offerings is what fuels the essence of Patikim ni Sta. Maria.

Vince Calderon - Wordsmith Extraordinaire

Vince Calderon, our Wordsmith Extraordinaire, turns each dining experience into a vivid narrative. Through his eloquent words, he captures the essence of the flavors, aromas, and stories that make Sta. Maria's culinary scene truly exceptional. Vince's linguistic prowess brings the Patikim ni Sta. Maria blog to life.

John Gabriel Guilalas - Visual Culinary Artist

As our Visual Culinary Artist, John Gabriel transforms the flavors of Sta. Maria into captivating visuals. With an artistic eye and a passion for photography, he skillfully captures the essence of each dish, making your virtual culinary journey a feast for the eyes. John Gabriel's images tell a story of taste and aesthetics.

Glaiza San Victores - Community Connector

Glaiza, our Community Connector, bridges the gap between Patikim ni Sta. Maria and our vibrant community. Through her dedication, she ensures that our blog not only showcases the town's culinary treasures but also fosters a sense of community pride. Glaiza brings people together through a shared love for food and culture.

Together, we form the creative force behind Patikim ni Sta. Maria, driven by a shared love for our town and a mission to celebrate its culinary excellence. Each blog post is a collaborative effort, blending our unique perspectives and talents to present you with an authentic and immersive culinary experience.

As we embark on this flavorful journey, we invite you to join us in exploring the tastes, traditions, and stories that make Sta. Maria a culinary haven. Thank you for being a part of our community, and we look forward to sharing many more delicious adventures with you on Patikim ni Sta. Maria!
