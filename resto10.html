Welcome to D's Secret Recipe

Welcome to D's Secret Recipe, a gastronomic treasure nestled in the heart of Guyong, Sta. Maria, Bulacan. Step into a world where each dish is a masterpiece, and every bite is a revelation of secret flavors crafted to perfection.

Experience Culinary Magic at D's Secret Recipe in Guyong, Sta. Maria, Bulacan!


Unveiling the Culinary Secrets:


Truffle Infused Pasta - Php 220: Indulge in the luxurious aroma of truffles with our Truffle Infused Pasta. A harmonious blend of al dente pasta and the earthy richness of truffle oil.

Grilled Salmon Delight - Php 350: Elevate your dining experience with the Grilled Salmon Delight. Perfectly grilled salmon fillet served with a medley of seasonal vegetables and our signature sauce.

Secret Recipe Chicken Wings - Php 180: Dive into the mystery of our Secret Recipe Chicken Wings. Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, and coated in a secret sauce that keeps you coming back for more.

Seafood Ramyun Delight - Php 220: Indulge in the oceanic symphony of flavors with our Seafood Ramyun Delight. Packed with fresh seafood and aromatic broth, it's a feast for the senses.

Delightful Surprises Beyond the Ordinary:


Signature Seafood Paella - Php 380: Immerse yourself in the rich flavors of our Signature Seafood Paella. A tantalizing fusion of saffron-infused rice, assorted seafood, and Spanish-inspired goodness.

Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake - Php 150: Conclude your culinary journey on a sweet note with our Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake. A divine creation that oozes velvety chocolate with every indulgent spoonful.

The Place

At D's Secret Recipe, we believe in creating more than just meals; we curate experiences. Our cozy ambiance, attentive service, and thoughtfully designed interiors provide the perfect backdrop for intimate gatherings, celebrations, and memorable moments.
