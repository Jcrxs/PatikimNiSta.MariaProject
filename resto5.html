Welcome to Diko's Farm Grill and Restaurant

Nestled in the scenic surroundings of Sta. Maria, Bulacan, Diko's Farm Grill and Restaurant invites you to embark on a gastronomic journey where farm-fresh ingredients meet culinary expertise.

Experience Culinary Delight at Diko's Farm Grill and Restaurant!


Farm-to-Table Goodness:


At Diko's, we take pride in offering a farm-to-table dining experience, ensuring that every dish reflects the freshness and quality of locally sourced ingredients. Our menu is a celebration of flavors inspired by the bountiful harvests of our very own farm.

Grilled Farm Fresh Platter - Php 350:
Indulge in a medley of freshly grilled vegetables and succulent meats, capturing the essence of farm goodness.

Diko's Special Bulalo - Php 280:
A comforting bowl of bulalo crafted with care, brimming with rich broth and tender beef, creating a soul-satisfying dish.

Farmhouse Pizza - Php 220:
Our unique take on pizza, topped with farm-fresh produce and premium cheeses for a delightful and cheesy bite.

Harvest Salad Bowl - Php 180:
A refreshing ensemble of crisp greens, vibrant vegetables, and house-made dressings, offering a burst of farm-fresh flavors.

The Place

Diko's Farm Grill and Restaurant is more than just a dining destination; it's an escape into the rustic charm of the countryside. Our cozy and welcoming ambiance is enhanced by the lush greenery surrounding the establishment, providing a tranquil setting for a delightful meal.





Visit us at:

Bypass Rd. Santa Clara, Sta. Maria, Bulacan (Near Waltermart infront of Shell Gas Station and Burger King)
