Welcome to FAB’s Burger

Welcome to a gastronomic haven where taste meets innovation – FAB’s Burger! Nestled in the heart of Luwasan, Sta. Maria, Bulacan, FAB’s Burger is a culinary destination that promises an explosion of flavors in every bite.

Indulge in Irresistible Flavors at FAB’s Burger in Luwasan, Sta. Maria, Bulacan!


Discover Our Mouthwatering Menu:


lassic Cheeseburger - Php 80:
A timeless favorite with a perfect blend of juicy beef, melted cheese, and fresh veggies.

FAB’s Special Burger - Php 120:
Elevate your burger experience with our signature blend of premium ingredients that make this burger truly special.

Cheesy Bacon Burger - Php 100:
Immerse yourself in the rich and savory goodness of crispy bacon combined with melted cheese.

Mushroom Melt Burger - Php 110:
For mushroom enthusiasts, indulge in the earthy flavors of sautéed mushrooms and melted cheese.

Spicy Chicken Burger - Php 90:
Add a kick to your taste buds with our spicy chicken patty topped with zesty sauces.

Perfectly golden and seasoned to complement your burger experience.

Crispy and flavorful, the ideal sidekick to your burger indulgence.

The Place

FAB’s Burger in Luwasan boasts a welcoming atmosphere, blending modern vibes with a touch of local charm. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or enjoying a leisurely meal, our cozy ambiance sets the stage for a delightful dining experience.



Visit us at:

Sitio Dulung Luwasan, Acacia St. Santa Clara, Santa Maria, Bulacan

Wednesday – Sunday 11:00 AM - 9:00 PM
