Welcome to Angie's Grill and Restaurant

Welcome to Angie's Grill and Restaurant, your go-to destination for a delightful culinary experience in the heart of Sta. Maria, Bulacan. We invite you to embark on a flavorful journey where every dish is a testament to our passion for great food and warm hospitality.

Indulge in Culinary Delights at Angie's Grill and Restaurant in Sta. Maria, Bulacan!


Angie's Grill and Restaurant Menu:



Appetizers:

Cheesy Garlic Bread - Php 90:
BA delightful combination of crusty bread, melted cheese, and garlic goodness.

Calamari Fritti - Php 150:
Crispy fried calamari rings served with zesty marinara sauce.

Grilled Specialties:


Angie's BBQ Platter - Php 350: A feast of grilled barbecue delights, including pork skewers, chicken inasal, and beef kebabs.

T-Bone Steak - Php 450: Juicy T-bone steak cooked to perfection, served with your choice of sides.

Seafood Sensations:


Grilled Bangus Belly - Php 180: Succulent milkfish belly marinated and grilled to perfection. Shrimp Scampi - Php 280: Flavorful shrimp sautéed in garlic, butter, and lemon, served with pasta.

Home-Style Comfort Food:


Chicken Adobo sa Gata - Php 220: A comforting twist on the classic adobo, cooked with coconut milk.

Sinigang na Baboy - Php 250: A Filipino sour tamarind-based pork soup, served with fresh vegetables.

Pasta Perfection:


Carbonara Supreme - Php 200: Creamy carbonara with bacon, mushrooms, and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.
Seafood Aglio Olio - Php 280: Spaghetti tossed in olive oil, garlic, and a medley of seafood.

Dessert Delights:


Mango Float - Php 120: Layers of graham crackers, mango slices, and creamy filling for a refreshing dessert.
Leche Flan - Php 80: A velvety caramel custard, a Filipino classic to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Beverages:


Angie's Iced Tea - Php 50: A refreshing blend of iced tea with a hint of citrus.
Fruit Shake - Php 90: A tropical mix of fresh fruits blended into a delightful shake.

The Place

At Angie's, we believe that dining is not just about food; it's about creating lasting memories in a cozy and inviting setting. Our restaurant exudes a welcoming atmosphere, making it the perfect place for family gatherings, friendly meet-ups, or romantic dinners.
