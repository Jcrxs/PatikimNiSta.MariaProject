Welcome to FIO's Sizzler!

Embark on a culinary journey with FIO's Sizzler, a hidden gem in Sta. Clara, Santa Maria, Bulacan, that's making waves with its enticing array of sizzling specialties. Nestled in the heart of the town, FIO's Sizzler promises a gastronomic experience like no other.

Sizzle into Flavorful Delights at FIO's Sizzler!

Here's a sneak peek into the sizzling symphony awaiting your taste buds:


Sizzling Sisig (Special) - 95php:
A sizzling sensation that captures the essence of Filipino comfort food.

Tapsilog - 95 php:
A classic favorite, marrying the rich flavors of tapa, sinangag (garlic fried rice), and itlog (egg).

Porksilog - 105php:
Succulent pork paired perfectly with sinangag and itlog for a hearty meal..

Chicksilog - 105php:
Indulge in the delightful combination of chicken, sinangag, and itlog.

Sizzling BBQ Liempo - 110php:
Grilled to perfection, this sizzling BBQ liempo promises a flavorful adventure.

Charonlaksilog - 105php:
A delightful mix of chorizo, longganisa, sinangag, and itlog for a taste explosion.

Sizzling Tofu - 100php:
A sizzling treat for our vegetarian friends, combining the goodness of tofu with savory flavors.

Sizzling Beef Pepper - 120php:
Tender beef slices infused with a burst of pepper goodness.

Daingsilog - 120php:
Experience the classic Filipino breakfast with crispy daing na bangus.

Lechon Kawali Silog - 120php:
Crispy lechon kawali paired with sinangag and itlog for a delightful meal.

Sizzling Hungarian (Sausage) - 115php:
Savor the spiciness of Hungarian sausage in a sizzling platter.

Sizzling Crispy Kare-Kare - 140php:
A unique twist to the traditional kare-kare, served sizzling and crispy.

Sizzling Chicken BBQ - 100php:
Juicy chicken barbecue, perfectly grilled and served hot.

Sizzling Bicol Express - 115php:
A sizzling rendition of the spicy Bicol Express, a true Filipino favorite.

Sizzling Beef Caldereta - 145php:
Experience the richness of beef caldereta served in a sizzling hot plate.

Liemposilog - 125php:
Enjoy the goodness of liempo with sinangag and itlog in this delightful combination.

At FIO's Sizzler, every dish tells a story of flavors that dance on your palate. Whether you're a sizzling sisig enthusiast or craving the comforting taste of tapsilog, FIO's Sizzler invites you to savor a symphony of sizzling delights that will leave you coming back for more. Come, join us at FIO's Sizzler, where every sizzle is a celebration of Filipino culinary artistry!

The Place

FIO's Sizzler offers more than just delectable dishes; it provides a cozy atmosphere where friends and family can gather. Our charming venue, adorned with warm tones and welcoming vibes, creates the perfect backdrop for your dining pleasure.



Visit us at:

Lambak St. - Lalakhan-Sta. Clara by-pass road. Near Fuel Hub and Starbucks.
