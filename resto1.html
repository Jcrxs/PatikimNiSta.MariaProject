Welcome to Sangyup Grden!

Sangyup Garden in Caypombo, Santa Maria, Bulacan, is a culinary oasis waiting to be explored. Tucked away in the heart of this charming town, Sangyup Garden offers a unique dining experience surrounded by lush greenery and a tranquil atmosphere.

Savor the Specialties at Sangyup Garden!

Indulge in a symphony of flavors with our specially crafted unlimited packages at Sangyup Garden. Each offering is a culinary masterpiece designed to elevate your dining experience to new heights. Explore our specialties:

Unlimited Pork Delight (PHP 249.00):

Immerse yourself in a pork lover's paradise with unlimited pork in three tantalizing flavors. Dive into an endless array of side dishes that perfectly complement the succulent pork. Satisfy your cravings with unlimited servings of steamed rice.

Pork & Chicken Extravaganza (PHP 299.00):

Experience a duet of flavors with unlimited pork and chicken, available in eight delectable varieties. Accompany your feast with an assortment of unlimited side dishes. Fill your plate with unlimited servings of perfectly steamed rice.

Ultimate Trio - Pork, Chicken & Beef Fiesta (PHP 349.00):

Embark on the ultimate feast with unlimited pork, chicken, and beef, each available in a staggering sixteen flavors. Elevate your dining experience with a serving of luscious steamed egg. Delight in a symphony of flavors with unlimited side dishes and rice.

Irresistible Add-Ons:

Unlimited Cheese Sauce (Php 50 per head) / Ala carte: Dive into the gooey goodness of melted cheese to enhance your barbecue experience.

Unlimited Iced Tea (Php 20 per head) / Ala carte: Quench your thirst with refreshing iced tea, the perfect accompaniment to your flavorful meal.

Steamed Egg (Php 100): Delight in the creamy goodness of our special steamed egg, a perfect addition to your feast.

Melona Ice Cream (Php 40): Conclude your dining adventure on a sweet note with our delightful Melona Ice Cream.

At Sangyup Garden, we invite you to explore the diverse and delectable specialties crafted to delight your senses. Join us for an unforgettable journey of flavors and culinary wonders!

The Place

The ambiance at Sangyup Garden is as delightful as the cuisine. Outdoor seating surrounded by beautiful gardens and soothing water features creates a perfect setting for romantic dinners, celebratory feasts, or casual gatherings with friends.



Visit us at:
